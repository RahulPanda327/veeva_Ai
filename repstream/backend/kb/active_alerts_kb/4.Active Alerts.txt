Active Alerts :
The Active Alerts module uses AI to continuously monitor territory performance and proactively identify risks or opportunities that require action.

1. Anomaly Detection
The system monitors prescription trends, competitor activity, payer changes, and HCP engagement patterns. When it detects unusual changes from normal behavior, it automatically generates alerts and assigns a priority level (Critical, High, or Medium).

Examples:
Competitive messaging changes
Sudden Rx volume decline
HCP awareness drop
Payer formulary updates

Business value:
Provides early warning signals before significant business impact occurs.
Helps teams react faster to market changes.


2. AI-Based Impact Analysis
For each alert, AI evaluates the potential business impact and quantifies the risk.

What it shows:
Number of affected HCPs
Territory reach
Covered lives impacted
Rx risk level
Access impact

Business value:
Helps managers understand the severity and scale of an issue.
Supports prioritization of high-impact alerts.


3. Recommended Actions & Next Best Action
Each alert is paired with an AI-recommended response and actionable next steps.

Examples:
Deploy approved counter-messaging to the field
View affected HCPs
Access support resources
Schedule follow-up calls
Dismiss or acknowledge alerts

Business value:
Enables immediate action directly from the platform.
Reduces response time and improves field effectiveness.
