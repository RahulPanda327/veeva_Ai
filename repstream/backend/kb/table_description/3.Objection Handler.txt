Objection Handler :
The Objection Handler module helps sales representatives prepare for common physician objections by analyzing past call conversations and recommending the most effective approved responses.

1. Call Transcript Ingestion :
The system automatically collects and stores call transcripts from field interactions across the territory.

Purpose:
Creates a central repository of sales conversations.
Provides the data needed for AI analysis.


2. NLP Analysis
AI uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze all call transcripts and identify recurring objection themes.

Examples of objections:
Concerns about side effects
Complex dosing schedules
Cost or reimbursement concerns

Purpose:
Detects trends that may not be obvious from individual calls.
Helps understand what physicians are consistently asking about.


3. Objection Classification
The system groups and ranks objections based on how often they occur.

What it shows:
Objection statement
Number of calls where it appeared
Detection period
Frequency label (High / Medium)

Purpose:
Helps reps focus on the most common barriers to adoption.
Prioritizes preparation for frequently encountered objections.


4. MLR-Approved Response Recommendations
For each objection, the platform recommends the most effective MLR-approved response based on historical performance.

What it includes:
Recommended response script
Supporting materials and SKU references
Historical success/conversion rate

Purpose:
Ensures compliant messaging.
Provides proven responses that have performed well in similar situations.
