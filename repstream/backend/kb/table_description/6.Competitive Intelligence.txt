Competitive Intelligence :
The Competitive Intelligence module continuously monitors competitor activity across the territory and provides early alerts on changes that could impact brand performance.

1. Competitor Anomaly & Trend Detection :
AI tracks competitor messaging, market share changes, and field activity in real time.

What it detects:
New competitor claims or messaging
Market share gains or losses
Changes in competitor rep call activity

Alert types:
AI Detected – New or unusual competitor activity identified by AI.
ML Trend – Gradual competitive trend developing over time.
Anomaly – Sudden statistical change that differs from normal behavior.

Business value:
Gives early visibility into competitive threats before they significantly impact the territory.
Helps teams respond proactively rather than reactively.


2. Counter-Strategy Recommendations
When available, the system provides approved counter-messaging or recommended actions.

Examples:
Reference supporting clinical evidence
Use approved competitive positioning materials
Notify payer or market access teams

Business value:
Helps reps respond quickly with compliant and evidence-based messaging.
Reduces time spent analyzing competitor activity manually.
