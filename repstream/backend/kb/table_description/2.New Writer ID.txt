New Writer ID :
The New Writer ID module helps sales representatives identify healthcare providers (HCPs) who are potential new prescribers of our brand and provides guidance on how to engage them effectively.

1. New Writer Detection
The system identifies HCPs who are actively prescribing competitor or generic products within the same therapeutic class but are not yet prescribing our brand.

What it shows:
Physician name and specialty
Organization/practice
First New Prescription (NBRx) date

Business value:
Finds high-potential conversion opportunities early.
Helps sales reps focus on physicians who are already treating relevant patients and writing prescriptions in the category.

2. Peer Network Matching
The platform analyzes professional relationships and peer networks to determine how closely a new writer is connected to existing prescribers of our brand.

What it shows:
Peer Match % score (e.g., 87%, 72%)

Business value:
Higher percentages indicate stronger connections to current brand writers.
Helps prioritize physicians who may be more receptive to peer-driven influence and product adoption.


3. AI-Generated Warm Approach
The system generates a personalized recommendation explaining why the physician is a good target and how to approach them.

What it includes:
Relevant peer connections
Suggested conversation entry points
Potential conversion opportunity indicators

Example:
Connected to Dr. Davidson and currently prescribing competitor products in the same class.

Business value:
Gives sales reps a tailored engagement strategy instead of a generic sales approach.
Increases the likelihood of meaningful conversations.


4. ICD-10 Diagnosis Matching
The system identifies diagnosis codes commonly associated with the physician's patients.

What it shows:
Relevant ICD-10 codes (e.g., Type 2 Diabetes, Hyperlipidemia)

Business value:
Confirms that the physician treats patients appropriate for the brand.
Ensures outreach is clinically relevant.


5. Competitive Prescribing Insights
Displays the physician's top prescriptions within the therapeutic class.

What it shows:
Competitor brands
Generic alternatives
Prescription volumes

Business value:
Reveals which products the physician currently prefers.
Helps sales reps understand where conversion opportunities exist.


6. Generate Approach Brief
Provides a detailed AI-generated call preparation document.

What it includes:
Physician profile
Prescribing behavior
Peer network insights
Suggested talking points
Recommended engagement strategy

Business value:
Reduces preparation time.
Helps sales reps conduct more informed and targeted interactions.
