Territory Prioritization :
Purpose
The Territory Prioritization module uses AI to help sales representatives identify which HCPs (Healthcare Professionals) should be contacted first and what action should be taken.

Key Terms
1. HCP (Healthcare Professional)
A doctor or healthcare provider being targeted by the sales team.

Examples:
Dr. Sarah Davidson
Dr. Michael Chen
Dr. Anjali Patel

2. Rx (Prescription)
Rx = Prescription
Represents the number of prescriptions written by an HCP for a product.

Example
Period	   Total Rx
Q4 2025	    35
Q1 2026	    47

Interpretation:
Prescriptions increased from 35 to 47.
Positive growth trend.
AI may assign higher priority.



3. HCP Priority Ranking :
AI assigns a priority level to each HCP.

Priority Levels:
Priority	      Meaning
High	         Immediate opportunity; should be contacted soon
Medium	         Moderate opportunity
Low	         Lower immediate value

Example:
HCP	               Priority
Dr. Sarah Davidson	High
Dr. Anjali Patel	Medium


4. AI-Generated Insights
AI creates a short summary explaining why the HCP is important and what action should be taken.

Example
Insight:
Rx volume increased 34% this quarter. Competitor brand share declining. High conversion potential.

Meaning:
Prescriptions are growing.
Competitor influence is decreasing.
Good opportunity for engagement.


5. Prescription Trend Analysis
Compares prescription volume across different periods.

Dashboard Fields :
Field	                         Meaning
Total Rx Q1	          Current quarter prescriptions
Total Rx Q4	          Previous quarter prescriptions
Last Rx	                  Most recent prescription date

Example
Metric	               Value
Total Rx Q1	        47
Total Rx Q4	        35

Result:
Growth = 34%
Positive trend


6. Segment
A business classification assigned to an HCP.

Common Segments
Segment	              Meaning
Target A	Highest-value HCPs
Target B	Medium-value HCPs
Target C	Lower-value HCPs


Example
HCP	                  Segment
Dr. Sarah Davidson	Target A
Dr. Anjali Patel	Target B
Important

Segment ≠ Priority

Segment	                                     Priority
Business classification	               AI-generated ranking
Usually static	                         Dynamic
Target A/B/C	                       High/Medium/Low

An HCP can be:
Segment = Target A
Priority = High

or

Segment = Target A
Priority = Medium
depending on current performance.


7. Weekly Target Recommendation
AI recommends how many HCPs should be contacted during the current week.

Example
This Week Target = 22

Meaning:
AI recommends contacting 22 HCPs this week.
Selection is based on priority ranking and opportunity score.




Flow :
HCP Data
(Prescriptions, Calls, Segments, Peer Network)
            |
            v
      AI Analysis
            |
            +--> Rx Trends
            +--> Growth Potential
            +--> Competitive Signals
            +--> Peer Network Insights
            |
            v
     Priority Ranking
(High / Medium / Low)
            |
            v
   AI Generated Insights
            |
            v
 Weekly Target Recommendation
            |
            v
      Rep Action Plan
