HCP Awareness Monitoring :
The HCP Awareness module tracks how engaged and informed healthcare providers (HCPs) are about our product and identifies early signs of declining awareness before it impacts prescribing behavior.

1. Average Awareness Score Trend :
The system measures the average awareness score across all HCPs in the territory and tracks it over time.

What it shows:
Territory-wide awareness trend (e.g., Jan → Feb → Mar → Apr)
Color indicators (Green → Amber → Red) showing declining engagement levels

Business value:
Provides an early warning when overall HCP awareness is dropping.
Helps teams identify when additional education or engagement campaigns may be needed.


2. NLP Analysis of At-Risk HCPs :
AI identifies individual HCPs whose awareness scores have declined significantly and analyzes the likely reason.

Examples of root causes:
Increased consumption of competitor content
Reduced interaction with field representatives
Changes in prescribing behavior
Lower engagement with educational materials

What it shows:
Awareness score and decline percentage
Prescribing patterns
Relevant ICD-10 focus areas
Activity insights explaining the decline

Business value:
Helps reps understand why awareness is dropping.
Enables targeted and personalized re-engagement efforts.


3. Recommended Action
The system provides immediate next steps such as Schedule Call for HCPs showing declining awareness.

Business value:
Allows reps to proactively reconnect with at-risk physicians.
Helps prevent future prescribing drift and loss of market share.
